## The Tool List from Kalamazoo X

Here is the list of tools that I mentioned (and some I forgot to mention) during my talk at Kalamazoo X.

1. [Basecamp](http://basecamphq.com/)
2. [AgileZen](http://www.agilezen.com/)
3. [Google docs](http://docs.google.com/)
4. [Google sites](http://sites.google.com/)
5. [Trac](http://trac.edgewall.org/)
6. [Bugzilla](http://www.bugzilla.org/)
7. [Tiddlywiki](http://www.tiddlywiki.com/)
8. [Pivotal Tracker](http://www.pivotaltracker.com/)
